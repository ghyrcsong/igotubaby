Checklist

Crib, Crib mattress, Bedding(blankets, top sheets, pillows, and bumper pads…), Bassinet, Night-light, Clothes storage, Nursery glider or rocking chair, Pacifiers, Toy basket, Mobile

Baby swing, Baby bouncer, Portable play yard, Toys(like simple rattles or books with high contrast patterns, soft toys that make sounds, stacking toys, push-pull toys…), Play mat

Burp cloths, Nursing cover, Receiving blankets, Nursing pillow(U-shaped cushy pillow), Bibs, Bottles and nipples, Breast pump, Formula, Bottle warmer, Milk storage bags, Bottle brush, Bottle sterilizer, Bottle-drying rack

Changing table, Changing pad, Diapers, Wipes, Washcloths, Diaper rash cream, Diaper pail

Swaddle blanket(During the first few weeks, you may wish to swaddle your baby, and a special blanket can be helpful. Although you can use a receiving blanket, there are other options available, including ones that have a hook-and-loop fastener for more convenience.), Pajamas, sleepers, or sleeping sacks, Undershirts and one-piece outfits, T-shirts, Leggings or stretchy pants, Sweater or jacket, Socks or booties, Hat with brim, Knit hat or cap, Bunting bag or snowsuit, Mittens, Special outfit(optional item)

Baby bath seat or tub, Shampoo, Soap, Cup(A plastic scoop or cup helps you gently pour water over your baby's hair or skin to rinse out the soap or shampoo), Towels, Washcloths, Baby moisturizer

Car sea, Stroller, Baby carrier or wrap, Diaper bag, Sun shade for car windows, Portable crib, Portable changing pad, Disposable diaper pail, Stroller rain cover

First-aid kit(basic items like a baby thermometer, tweezers, antibiotic ointment, adhesive bandages, and petroleum jelly…), Bulb syringe, Nail clippers or a soft emery board, Soft-bristled hairbrush, Sunscreen, Gentle detergent

White-noise machine or music player, Humidifier, Blackout shades
